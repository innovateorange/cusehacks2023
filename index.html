CREATE SOMETHING AWESOME!

December 2nd and 3rd on the third floor of the Schine Student Center

Download sponsorship package

An invention marathon!

Students from all over Syracuse University will come together to create an app, website, or whatever tech project you want to create in the 24 hours that you have.

Why should I come?

There will be games, food, and prizes! Build something or break something, you'll have fun meeting new people, working with friends, and gaining new skills!

Who can come?

Anyone can come! No matter if you're a hackathon veteran or if you have never heard of one before, you'll have a great time!
