What is Cusehacks

CuseHacks is an invention marathon. Students come together to create an app, website, or whatever tech project you want to create in the 24 hours that you have. It's very beginner friendly and we encourage students with any level of experience to sign up.

Why should I come

Throughout the weekend, you can use your imagination to make anything you want with the help of your amazing peers and impressive mentors. There will be games, food, and prizes! Build something or break something, we just want you to have a great experience meeting new people, working with friends, and gaining new skills!
